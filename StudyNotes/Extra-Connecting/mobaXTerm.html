An easy way to connect to the server, that works on all versions of Windows, and does not make changes to your machine, is Moba XTerm. This includes a nice terminal emulator, several commands (including ssh and scp) and an XWindows server.

To log on to our server:

  • Download Mobaxterm, from okaram(3.0, current as of May 2011) or Mobatek's site. You may want to save this on your local machine so you do not have to download it again.
  • Run the downloaded executable
  • Type: ssh -X me@okaram.spsu.edu Replace me with your username (usually same as spsu email). Notice that the X is capitalized, -X.
  • You will be asked for your password; after typing it correctly, you should be logged on to the server (the prompt will change, and it should mention okaram; also, moba will display a list of files on the right hand side)
  • After you've logged on to the server, you can run commands; these commands will be executed ON THE SERVER, not on your local machine. Some of the most useful commands are:
    • bluefish -- a text editor (remember, it is running on the server, so files will be saved to the server; anything saved on your public_html folder should be visible through the web)
    • g++ -- C++ compiler.
    • psql -- client for postgresql DBMS
    • sqlplus -- client for oracle DBMS
